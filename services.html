Our Services

Comprehensive Construction & Structural Engineering Solutions

IDtruss Ltd offers a complete range of construction and engineering services designed to meet the unique needs of residential, commercial, and specialized projects. Our team leverages decades of combined expertise to deliver exceptional results.

🏗️ Structural Engineering

Our structural engineers design safe, efficient, and innovative structures that meet all building codes and safety standards. From initial concept through final construction, we ensure structural integrity and optimal performance.

What We Offer:

  • Building design and analysis
  • Load calculations and stress testing
  • Foundation design
  • Compliance with building codes and standards
  • 3D modeling and simulations
  • Engineering documentation and permits
📐

🏠 Roof Truss Design

Specialized in designing high-performance roof truss systems for residential and commercial buildings. Our engineered trusses provide superior load-bearing capacity, cost efficiency, and design flexibility.

What We Offer:

  • Custom roof truss design
  • Structural analysis and optimization
  • CAD drawings and specifications
  • Engineered truss connections
  • Cost-effective span solutions
  • Installation guidance and support
🏘️

🏘️ Residential Construction

We specialize in building quality residential homes that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal. From single-family homes to multi-unit developments, we deliver projects that exceed homeowner expectations.

What We Offer:

  • Custom home construction
  • Multi-family residential projects
  • Site preparation and foundation work
  • Complete framing and finishing
  • Quality inspections and testing
  • Project management and coordination
🏡

🏢 Commercial Construction

Our commercial construction expertise spans office buildings, retail spaces, industrial facilities, and specialized structures. We manage complex projects with precision, keeping timelines and budgets on track.

What We Offer:

  • Commercial building construction
  • Industrial facility development
  • Retail and office spaces
  • Specialized industrial structures
  • Large-scale project management
  • Schedule and budget optimization
🏛️

🔧 Renovation & Remodeling

Transform your existing space with expert renovation and remodeling services. We handle everything from kitchen and bathroom updates to complete structural overhauls, all while maintaining structural integrity.

What We Offer:

  • Interior remodeling
  • Kitchen and bathroom renovations
  • Structural modifications
  • Building system upgrades
  • Historic preservation work
  • Minimal disruption scheduling
