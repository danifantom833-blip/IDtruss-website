How long does the initial consultation take?

Initial consultations typically last 30-45 minutes. We discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget to determine if we're the right fit for your needs.

Yes! We provide detailed quotes at no obligation. After discussing your project, we'll develop a comprehensive proposal with pricing and timelines.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small renovations may take weeks, while large commercial projects can take 12-24 months. We'll provide detailed scheduling during planning.
